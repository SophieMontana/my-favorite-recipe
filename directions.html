Directions to Make
Vegan No-Bake Cookies

  1. Heat almond butter (or peanut butter), brown sugar, coconut oil and almond milk in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring until the oil and sugar have melted.
  2. Let cool slightly.
  3. Drop the dough by tablespoonfulls onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. Press into 2-inch circles.
  4. Refrigerate until firm, about 2 hours.

Tips

To make ahead: Refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 5 days

People with Celiac disease or gluten senitivity should use oats that are labeled "gluten-free", as oats are often cross-contaminated with wheat and barley.
